================================================================================
VALIDATION RULES
================================================================================

FRONTEND (Client-side) - Zod Schema:
  Name:
    - Minimum: 2 characters
    - Maximum: 150 characters
    
  Email:
    - Must match email format (RFC 5322)
    - Minimum: 5 characters
    - Maximum: 150 characters
    
  Subject (optional):
    - Maximum: 255 characters
    
  Message:
    - Minimum: 20 characters
    - Maximum: 5000 characters
    
  Website (honeypot):
    - Must be empty (bot detection)

BACKEND (Server-side) - Express-validator:
  Name:
    - Required
    - Trimmed
    - Maximum: 150 characters
    
  Email:
    - Required
    - Valid format checked
    - Normalized to lowercase
    - Maximum: 150 characters
    
  Subject:
    - Optional
    - Trimmed
    - Maximum: 255 characters
    
  Message:
    - Required
    - Trimmed
    - Minimum: 20 characters
    - Maximum: 5000 characters
    
  Website:
    - Empty only (honeypot)
